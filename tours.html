Explore Our Town with Re-cycle Tours:

Choose your adventure and discover our town from a unique perspective!

  • The Historic Heart Tour:
    Pedal through the charming old town, visiting key historical landmarks and learning about our rich heritage.
    Duration: approx. 2 hours
    Distance: approx. 8 km
    Difficulty: Easy

  • The Scenic Riverside Ride:
    Enjoy a leisurely cycle along our beautiful river, taking in picturesque views and local wildlife.
    Duration: approx. 3 hours
    Distance: approx. 12 km
    Difficulty: Easy to Moderate

  • The Hidden Gems Expedition:
    Discover the local secrets and off-the-beaten-path treasures that only the locals know.
    Duration: approx. 4 hours
    Distance: approx. 15 km
    Difficulty: Moderate
